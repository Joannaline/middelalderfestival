VARDE MEDIEVAL FESTIVAL

31st AUGUST - 3th SEPTEMBER

When autocity and cosiness go hand in hand

Authenticity:

Are you also curious about how to live back in the Middle Ages and are you ready to go back in time? At the Varde Medieval Festival you get the chance to come close to when the nutmeg stands ready to teach you to paint flour, the smell of bonfire draws into your newly made monk cutter and the musicians offer dance.

Sharing knowledge:

It can be difficult to get into what the Middle Ages are and how they lived. Therefore, the importance of knowledge sharing is paramount when you visit the Varde Medieval Festival. Whether you are a child or an adult does not matter. Here you will find knowledge sharing at children's height and more profound for the hardcore.

Community:

To us, community plays a huge role. Both you feel like you're in a larger community when you visit us, but also as a whole. When you come to the Varde Medieval Festival we are many who have worked hard to give you a nice and enjoyable experience. Thank you for contributing to the community.

Varde Medieval Festival

We are proud to present the theme of the Vardes Medieval Festival in 2019 which is "Rich and Poor" We look forward to sharing this amazing experience with you, focusing on community, knowledge sharing and authenticity. The festival is organized in collaboration with Vardes Kultur og Fritid og Kommune.

Medieval party

Eat as a king on Saturday evening, where the big party dinner is packed with a special piglet with accessories and cold supplies. More information comes later ...

Make your own monk cutter

Come past Nicolaus on Friday between 10-17, where you can make your very own monk cushion. He tells and shows how they were made and how it was to be poor in the Middle Ages.

Medieval Weeping School

Boys and Girls - Meet up to Knight Edward's Cinderella School! Here you learn everything about discipline, honor and weaponry to become a true knight. The day ends with a battle against each other on life and death.

Drink yourself to Knight

On the medieval market, you can be knighted! You just have to drink 5 ice-cold fads. Then you turn to Knight of the round table.
